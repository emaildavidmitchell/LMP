Alphabetical list of part-of-speech tags used in the Penn Treebank Project:



1.	CC	Coordinating conjunction – e.g., and, but, for, nor, or, so, yet

3.	DT	Determiner – e.g., whose, which

5.	FW	Foreign word

7.	JJ	Adjective – describes a noun

12.	NN	Noun, singular or mass

13.	NNS	Noun, plural

14.	NNP	Proper noun, singular

15.	NNPS	Proper noun, plural

17.	POS	Possessive ending – this is the apostrophe “s” that means something belong’s to someone – e.g., Picasso’s influence. 

18.	PRP	Personal pronoun – e.g., he, she, they

19.	PRP$	Possessive pronoun – e.g., his, her

27.	VB	Verb, base form – e.g., run, write, paint, marry

28.	VBD	Verb, past tense – e.g., ran, wrote, painted, married

29.	VBG	Verb, gerund or present participle – e.g., running, writing, painting, marrying

30.	VBN	Verb, past participle – e.g., run, written, painted, married
